    Jao Silva Soares, Ph.D.

    PI, Assistant Professor, Mechanical and Nuclear Engineering

    Dr. Soares studied at Instituto Superior Tecnico in Lisbon, Portugol with a degree in Aerospace Engineering Bachelor’s and Master's degree til 2002. He pursued his education at Texas A&M University College Station with a degree in Mechanical Engineering PhD til 2008. Post-doctoral from ‘09-’10, Dr. Soares continued with Politecnico di Milano, Italy in Mathematics. From 2011-12, he furthered Biomedical Engineering at Stony Brook University in New York for post-doctoral. From 2013-15, he was a research associate and from 2016-17 a research scientist for the Institute for Computational Engineering and Sciences, University of Texas at Austin. From 2017 - present, Dr. Soares has been an Assistant Professor with the Department of Mechanical and Nuclear Engineering, Virginia Commonwealth University. Dr. Soares has contributed to science multiple times, such as polymer-degradation and erosion, shear-induced platelet activation, whole-heart modeling and cardiovascular bioengineering, and engineered tissue growth and remodeling.
